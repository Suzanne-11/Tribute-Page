Here's a timeline of Mother Teresa's life

  • Aug 26, 1910 - Mother Teresa is born.

  • Feb 4, 1918 - Mother Teresa's father Dies.

  • Feb 20, 1922 - Mother Teresa wants a Religious Life.

  • Feb 20, 1928 - Mother Teresa joins the Loretta Sisters.

  • Feb 20, 1929 - Mother Teresa reaches India.

  • May 21, 1931 - Agnes Turns to Teresa.

  • Sep 9, 1937 to Dec 20, 1946 - Life in Calcutta.

  • Sep 10, 1946 - The Call Within The call.

  • Apr 23, 1952 - Home for the Dying

  • Feb 20, 1980 - Bharath Ratna for Mother Teresa

  • Feb 20, 1991 - Battling with pneumonia

  • Feb 20, 1994 - Golden Honor of the Nation

  • Nov 20, 1996 - Health Problems

  • Sep 19, 1997 - Mother Teresa passes Away
